GAMELOFT BARCELONA

Jordi Balfegó

Located in the heart of Barcelona, Gameloft Barcelona has been developing benchmark games thanks to outstanding and highly talented people since 2006. Over the years, the studio has developed in titles such as Despicable Me: Minion Rush and participated in the production of global Ips. Now working in Asphalt 9: Legends and Disney Speedstorm, their games have always been fueled by the best franchises on the planet and downloaded by billions of players. The main pillars with which over 230 studio members work are always around diversity, art and out-of-the-box culture.

Jordi Balfegó, Animation Lead at Gameloft Barcelona, has twenty years of experience in the video game industry as a game developer and supervisor of animation. His track record includes several multimillion-dollar games like the Assassin’s Creed series, collaborations with Disney and Nintendo, and some excursions to other entertainment areas like J.A. Bayona’s “A Monster Calls” or Game of Thrones franchise. An advocate for videogames as the most evolved form of animation media, Jordi embraces the challenge of creating interactive characters that really fulfill the meaning of animation as “embodying an anima” into nonliving entities.
